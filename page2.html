Tattu

Tattu Restaurant & Bar offering diners a culinary experience like no other in the city. Located on Gartside street, Spinningfields, the restaurant's menu embraces traditional Asian flavours and delivers contemporary Chinese cuisine with delicate dim sum, stunning presentation and distinctive flavour combinations.
The cosmopolitan interior provides the ideal setting for bot social occasions and corporate entertainment!

Rudy's Neopolitan

Rudys is an independent Neopolitan pizzeria where you will be welcomed with a relaxed restaurant environment with chefs passionate on following the traditions and artistry of pizza from Naples - birthplace of pizza.
The dough is made on site everyday and cooked for just 60 seconds in a wood fired pizza oven. There are a large variety of pizza choices available accommodatingboth vegetarians and vegans as well as having the choice to create your very own pizza choice.

Platzki Restaurant

Discover the unique concept of Platzki a cosy restuarant with aninteresting and delicious take on traditional Polish cuisine. The menu changes twice a week so there's always something new to try.
Platzki is great for anyone who is keen to try polish cuisine or for those wanting to recall flavours from their childhood.
